Second Blog

Posted on 2022-03-10 from the blog HQ.

Howdy blog browser. Welcome to the ssssecond blog on my blog. This one has a snake theme. I decided my second blog would be unrelated and be about my fear of snakes. Ever since I was a child I have been afraid of snakes. There are two specific instances I think that really catapulted this fear forward into my life.

Well the first run in with a snake I had was when I was a child. I would say about 5 years old. We lived in the countryside and had a snake find its way into our house. What my mother decided to do was cover it with a bucket and close the door to the room it was in. Then we would wait until my father came home. Well this is what we did. We had it under a bucket and shut the door. Hours later when my dad came home from work he entered the room with the snake, while we all fearfully watched from outside the doorway. He approached the bucket slowly, hand hovering over top. In his other hand he wore a glove and was ready to grab the beast. As he lifted the bucket up from the ground, we all stood agasp. The snake was gone.

My second encounter with a snake in my life would be a few years later. This time it was a beast of a different nature. A water snake. I was about 9 years old at the time. We had rented a cottage on the lake in the Muskokas of Ontario. As I sat with my knees in the sand, water ebbing over me, suddenly a water snake appeared in the sand. Before I could react to what I had just seen, the serpent slithered its way across my legs and into the water. Its head bobbing above the water caused my skin to crawl. This foul limbless creature had invaded my personal space and I would not forget it.
